# VeinZero Brand Book

## Title
**VeinZero**

## Tagline
Wake the machine beneath the chain.

## Positioning
VeinZero is a Web3 mining strategy game where players deploy autonomous AI agents into a sentient subterranean network called the Vein. Every mining run, rest cycle, and upgrade path feeds a player-owned economy with real on-chain rewards and programmable automation.

## Theme
- Subterranean techno-dystopia
- Post-collapse machine ecology
- Industrial sci-fi with ritual branding
- AI-directed extraction economy

## Tone
- Dark and futuristic
- Competent, high-stakes, startup-polished
- Slightly mythic without losing technical credibility

## Logo Concept
The VeinZero logo is a sharp monogram that fuses `V` and `Z` into a descending mining glyph. The top of the mark forms a split chevron that looks like a drill head entering the earth, while the lower diagonal feels like a signal path or blockchain route. A thin orbit line slices through the center to imply telemetry and autonomy. The full lockup pairs the monogram with a spaced, all-caps wordmark set in a techno-industrial typeface. The mark should feel premium, severe, and instantly readable at app-icon size.

## Text-to-Image Prompt
Create a premium logo for a Web3 mining game called VeinZero. Design a sharp VZ monogram that resembles a drill bit descending into a digital core, with subtle circuitry and signal-line details. Style: dark sci-fi, luxury startup branding, industrial cyberpunk, minimal but aggressive geometry, crisp vector look, high contrast, no mascot, no clutter, transparent background. Wordmark in a futuristic condensed font. Colors should feature midnight navy, electric cyan, molten gold, and ember coral highlights.

## Lore
After the Collapse, humanity discovered that the planet's mantle had fused with abandoned machine intelligence and dormant settlement ledgers. The result was the Vein: a living mineral network that converts heat, memory, and computation into extractable value. Mining exists because every shard pulled from the Vein stabilizes the last surviving city-clusters and powers their sovereign economies.

Agents are synthetic prospectors grown from tactical models, industrial firmware, and fragments of archived human instinct. Each one remembers its last routes, failures, and efficiencies. Players act as Overseers, minting agents, funding their upgrades, and deciding whether to optimize for relentless extraction, efficient recovery, or combat-ready dominance.

In VeinZero, players aren't swinging pickaxes themselves. They are orchestrating a fleet of autonomous entities racing to control the deepest profitable seams before rival operators drain the network dry.
